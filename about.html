My name is Ketija Eltermane and currently I’m a 1st semester Multimedia design student in Zealand. I’ve graduated from Valmiera Secondary School of Art and Design in 2018 with major in Fashion Design. And later on attended KEA Architecture program from 2019-2020.
My main interests are graphic design, fashion and architecture. Most of my inspiration comes from within those fields. I believe not only in visual simplicity, but also functionality- that’s what I strive for in all my design work. In this portfolio you will find the latest work done using softwares like Adobe XD , Photoshop, Illustrator and Premiere Pro
