Project goal was to develop a concept and sales platform for fictitious car brand. Our group focused on finding a solution for the current car surplus and car emission pollution. We developed BMC to refine our target group, performed user tests and scenarios. The final product was eco-friendly completely solar panel energy powered and affordable car for families and travellers. The website is coded with HTML and CSS

process

For developing this project, we started with desk-research about the current problems in the car industry. Then moved on to brainstorm phase, where the idea was refined. Our task was to conduct interviews and produce personas, scenarios to understand more if the product will be useful. Thereafter , I was responsible for SWOT competitive analysis to understand our brands potential. Once the main idea was clear, we moved onto design phase with sketches and wireframes.

I was responsible for the XD Prototype production (click on the picture).

Second part of the project required to develop SoMe content strategy to help us to establish strong brand identity and presence.
According to our BMC and content calendar, my group member and I developed three Instagram posters to draw more attention and traffic to our site/brand.
