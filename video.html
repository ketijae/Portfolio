TThe goal was to produce and film a short video portrait of my schoolmate. To Develop the concept I used tools such as brainstorm, mood-board , storyboard. The video was shot with Sony Alpha 7 and edited in Premiere Pro

process

I conducted a brainstorm on what and how should I show the personality of my schoolmate. My aim was to show the audience one quality about her, but leave the rest to decide themselves- how they perceive her and what they feel while watching the film. To help to refine the idea, I produced a moodboard to capture the idea more clearly
